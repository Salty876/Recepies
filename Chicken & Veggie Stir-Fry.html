Chicken & Veggie Stir-Fry

Getting takeout is a delectable delicacy. And, with this simple chicken veggie stir fry recipe, you can bring the magic of Chinese takeout into your own home. Depending on what you have in your fridge, you can change either the protein or vegetables. The basic sauce has a strong taste profile that will pull the dish together in any situation.

The ingredients

The sauce

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • ⅓ cup reduced sodium soy sauce(80 mL)
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 cup chicken broth(240 mL)
  • ¼ cup flour(30 g)

The rest

  • 1 lb chicken breast(455 g), cubed
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 lb broccoli florets(455 g)
  • 8 oz mushroom(225 g), sliced
  • 3 tablespoons oil, for frying

Instructions

    1.
    1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    2.
    Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in the same pan and add the mushrooms. When the mushrooms begin to soften, add the broccoli florets and continue to stir-fry until cooked. Set aside the cooked mushrooms and broccoli from the pan.
    3.
    Add 1 tablespoon of oil to the pan and sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant. Add the remaining sauce ingredients and stir until smooth.
    4.
    Return the chicken and veggies to the saucy pan and cook through, stirring occasionally.
    5.
    Serve with hot rice or noodles.
    6.
    Enjoy!
